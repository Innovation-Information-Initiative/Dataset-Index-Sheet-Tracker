This is the web version of the I3 Open Datasets Index, a collection of innovation datasets, and related tools, platforms and resources used by the broader innovation research community. In order to contribute to this site, you can edit either our google sheet (updates made to the sheet will take a couple of minutes to display), or make a pull request to our GitHub repository directly.
