--- title: I³ Open Innovation Dataset Index layout: main ---

This is the web version of the I³ Open Innovation Datasets Index – a collection of innovation datasets, and related tools, platforms and resources used by the broader innovation research community. You can contribute to this site, either by can editing our google sheet (updates made to the sheet will take a couple of minutes to display), or by making a pull request to our GitHub repository directly.

You can use the search bar to explore the datasets, or browse the full list directly, or explore the list of tools.
